Artist Name

Biography: Ben&Ben is a Filipino indie folk-pop band from Manila, formed in 2016 by twin brothers Paolo and Miguel Benjamin Guico (lead vocals and acoustic guitars). Initially known as The Benjamins, they expanded into a 9-piece ensemble and adopted their current name.

Discography: Ben&Ben has recorded and written material for two studio albums and one extended play (EP). They have collaborated with other artists and featured on songs on their respective albums.
